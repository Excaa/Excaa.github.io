What?

We are creating a radio show about Demoscene! Show will be broadcast on the internet @ www.wappuradio.fi and on the air around Tampere region on frequency 106,4 MHz.

The show will feature many awesome tracks and talking about the demoscene. We will be filled with nostalgia as this marks the fifth year on air for Sceneradio!

Show will mainly be in Finnish, but there might be some interviews in English. Music will be epic and awesome. If you want your music to be played on the program, please fill in this permit form.

The show is brought to you by Vuokko, Spiikki, Poro and Exca.

When?

Tune your internet tubes to www.wappuradio.fi in the hours of TBD or listen to us around Tampere region on the radio 106,4 MHz.
