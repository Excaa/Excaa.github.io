What?

We are creating a radio show about Demoscene to be aired all over Tampere with 200 watts of delivery power! Show will also be broadcast on the internet @ www.wappuradio.fi.

The show will feature many awesome tracks, interviews and talking about the demoscene.

Show will mainly be in Finnish, but there might be some interviews in English. Music will be epic and awesome. If you want your music to be played on the program, please fill in this permit form.

The show is brought to you by Vuokko, Spiikki, Poro and Exca.

When?

Tune your radios to 101,6 MHz in the hours of 18-21 UTC (21-24 EEST) at 20th of April or listen at www.wappuradio.fi.
