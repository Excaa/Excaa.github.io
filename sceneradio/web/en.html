What?

We are creating a radio show about Demoscene! Show will be broadcast on the internet @ www.wappuradio.fi and on the air around Tampere region on frequency 101,6 MHz.

The show will feature many awesome tracks and talking about the demoscene. We will be filled with nostalgia as this marks the 10th year on air for Sceneradio!

Show will mainly be in Finnish, but there might be some interviews in English. Music will be epic and awesome. If you want your music to be played on the program, please fill in this permit form.

The show is brought to you by Vuokko, Spiikki, Poro and Exca.

When?

Tune your internet tubes to www.wappuradio.fi in the hours of 22 until 01 or listen to us around Tampere region on the radio at frequency 101,6 MHz on saturday 19.4.2025

Competition?

Sceneradio will host a music competition during radio show. Entries will be played during the show on saturday 19.4.2025 at around 23:00.

Participate to the competition by sending your music to sceneradio@googlegroups.com with title 'Music Compo - artist - song name' before saturday 19.4.2025 17:00 UTC (19:00 EEST). (if you need more time, please contact us)

Rules:

  • Entry must be unreleased
  • Completely own production. Covers are not allowed.
  • Maximum playtime of 3 minutes. Longer entries will be faded out at the time limit.
  • Artist will permit Wappuradio the rights to play and distribute the entry.
  • Sceneradio retains the right to not play the entry.
